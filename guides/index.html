FAQ

Quick answers for common actor questions.

What is the best self tape app for actors?
The best self tape app for actors is one that handles recording, slates, organization, review, and delivery in one place. That is the problem iT Factor is designed to solve on iPhone.
How do you film a professional self tape on iPhone?
Keep the setup simple: clean framing, soft even light, reliable audio, and a camera position that lets performance stay central. The more your workflow reduces friction, the more professional the tape feels.
What should be included in a self-tape slate?
A slate usually includes your name and whatever details casting specifically requests, such as height, representation, or role. The right slate is the one that follows the instructions exactly and feels calm on camera.
How do casting directors prefer self tapes to be delivered?
Casting wants clean exports, clear file names, manageable file sizes, and a tape that opens without friction. Delivery quality signals professionalism before anyone presses play.
Can I organize auditions and takes in one app?
Yes. A purpose-built self tape workflow can hold projects, slates, notes, ratings, review, and exports in one place so actors are not stitching together five separate tools.
