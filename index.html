Founder Note

Founder Note

Created by casting. Built for actors.

Hi, I'm Kevin. Before I built this app, I worked in production and casting on the other side of the camera. I designed and set up the studio, worked with the best industry tools, and learned exactly what makes a submission feel professional.

I also learned what instantly makes a self-tape feel amateur, even when the talent is there. That gap stayed with me.

Most of the tools casting uses behind the scenes are not available to actors. So I built them.

Self Tape Studio was born from a simple belief: actors deserve the same level of professional workflow that production uses.

For years, I watched talented performers lose momentum not because of their craft, but because technology failed them. Glitchy apps. Corrupted files. Audio issues. Exports that did not match what was recorded.

When you are recording for something that could change your career, your software should never be the weak link.

So I built the tool I wish every actor had. Not flashy. Not bloated. No built-in shortcuts. Built to work under pressure.

Every feature in iT Factor exists because I have seen the real-world need for it, from casting rooms to production offices to living rooms turned into last-minute studios.

You bring the talent. We'll handle the workflow.

If you're here early, that means something to me. There is not a big corporation behind this, just someone who cares deeply about getting it right. Think of me as your first connection inside this app. If something can be better, I'm working on it. If something breaks, I care.

If this app helps you book something, that is the mission. Because at the end of the day, it is about your IT factor.

Kevin Founder, Self Tape Studio
