Privacy Policy

iT Factor — Self Tape Studio
Effective Date: April 5, 2026
Developer: Kevin Barrett (Apple Developer Program — Individual)
Contact: support@itfactor.studio

1. Overview

iT Factor — Self Tape Studio (“STS,” “we,” “us,” or “our”) is a professional self-tape recording and audition workflow application.

This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your information. STS is designed to minimize data collection and does not sell or monetize personal data.

2. Information We Collect

We collect only the information necessary to provide and improve STS.

A. Profile Information (if provided)

If you choose to create and manage a profile within the app, we may collect:

  • Name or stage name
  • Optional profile details you choose to provide (e.g., representation information, union status, sizing information)

Providing profile details is voluntary.

B. Content You Create

STS allows you to create and manage media content, including:

  • Video recordings
  • Audio recordings
  • Photos or keyframes
  • Project metadata (scene names, notes, ratings, tags)

Your recordings and creative content remain your property.

If iCloud sync is enabled, this data is stored using Apple’s CloudKit infrastructure under your Apple ID.

We do not access or review your recordings except when necessary to provide technical support and only if you explicitly request assistance.

C. Device & Diagnostic Information

We may collect limited technical information such as:

  • Device type
  • Operating system version
  • App version
  • Crash reports and diagnostic logs

This information is used solely to improve app stability and performance.

D. Subscription & Payment Information

All purchases are processed by Apple through the App Store.

We do not receive or store your credit card or payment details.

Apple may provide us with limited information such as:

  • Subscription status
  • Product purchased
  • Transaction identifiers

This information is used only to verify entitlements and enable Pro features.

Pro access is determined by your App Store subscription status on the Apple ID used for the purchase, not by whether you choose local mode or Sign in with Apple inside the app.

E. Account & Storage Modes

STS supports both local-only use and Sign in with Apple with optional iCloud sync.

  • If you continue without iCloud, your app session and profile data stay on this device unless you later enable iCloud features.
  • If you sign in with Apple and enable iCloud, supported profile and sync data may be stored in your iCloud account.

F. Permissions

STS may request access to:

  • Camera (to record video)
  • Microphone (to record audio)
  • Photo Library add-only access or Files access (to save or import media)
  • Notifications (to send audition reminders and related status updates)
  • Calendar write-only access (to add in-person audition sessions to your calendar without reading existing events)
  • Face ID / device passcode authentication if you enable the optional app lock feature

You may revoke permissions at any time through iOS Settings. Some features may not function properly if permissions are denied.

G. Website Analytics

Our website uses analytics to understand site usage, improve the website experience, and refine the marketing experience.

3. How We Use Information

  • Provide and operate STS features
  • Save and organize your projects
  • Sync data across devices (if iCloud sync is enabled)
  • Verify subscriptions and enable Pro features
  • Send optional audition reminders and due-date notifications
  • Add in-person audition sessions to your calendar when you request it
  • Protect app access with optional Face ID / passcode lock settings
  • Improve performance and fix bugs
  • Understand website usage and improve the website and marketing experience
  • Respond to support inquiries

We do not sell, rent, or share personal information for advertising or marketing purposes.

4. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

If you are located in the EEA, United Kingdom, or similar jurisdictions, our legal bases include:

  • Performance of a contract: Providing the app and its features
  • Legitimate interests: Improving stability, security, and performance
  • Compliance with legal obligations where required

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell personal information.

We may share information only in the following circumstances:

  • With Apple (subscription processing and iCloud services)
  • To comply with legal obligations
  • To protect rights and prevent fraud
  • In connection with a business transfer

6. Data Storage & Retention

If cloud sync is disabled, your data is stored locally on your device.

If iCloud sync is enabled, your data is stored securely using Apple’s CloudKit under your Apple ID.

7. Your Privacy Rights (GDPR & CCPA)

Depending on your location, you may have rights including:

  • Access your personal information
  • Request correction
  • Request deletion
  • Data portability
  • Restrict or object to processing

California residents have the right to know what information is collected and request deletion. We do not sell personal information.

To exercise rights, contact: support@itfactor.studio

8. Data Deletion

You may delete locally stored data by deleting the app.

If iCloud sync is enabled, manage stored data through your Apple ID iCloud settings.

9. Children’s Privacy

STS is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

10. International Users

Your information may be processed in the United States or other jurisdictions where service providers operate.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will update the Effective Date when changes are made.
